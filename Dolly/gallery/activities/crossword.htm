Why include crosswords?

Crosswords provide opportunities for learners to practise and rehearse learning content in a fun way. Particularly useful for areas that have large specific vocabulary requirements, crosswords provide opportunities for learners to familiarise themselves with vocabulary and their definitions. Often this can act as a form of self assessment, providing feedback and assisting in the construction of subject specific knowledge.

Integrating crosswords

Design steps

  • consider which parts of the course content could be practised or reinforced through crosswords
  • compile a list of vocabulary learners need to know
  • write clues which place the words in context
  • prepare student support materials.

Assessment

Crosswords can be used for both self assessment and summative assessment. A novel way to use crosswords involves giving learners the finished crosswords and asking them to write the clues. This is generally a more demanding task than answering the questions.

Technical notes

There are many software tools that can be used to create and publish crosswords for the web. Some are free and are easy for the novice to use. Others, like Flash, require higher level skills and are expensive to purchase and develop. See the Resources section below.

Skills

Usually the development of online crosswords is an option for novice developers using some of the free or low cost software products.

Tools and resources

  • Hot Potatoes
    Hot Potatoes is a software tool that creates online interactive execises, such as:
    • quizzes (true/false, multiple choice)
    • drag and drop activities
    • crosswords
    • cloze activities.
    The final product is a web page that can be then incorporated into any online product or emailed to recipients. Hot Potatoes is free for educational use. Some restrictions apply.
    http://www.halfbakedsoftware.com/index.php
  • Eclipse Crossword
    EclipseCrossword is a fast, easy and free way to create crossword puzzles in minutes.
    http://www.eclipsecrossword.com/
  • Web Crossword Creator
    Creates free printable crosswords that can be completed online. User editable with no links, no advertising and no copyright notices.
    http://www.web-crossword-maker.com/
  • Instant Online Crossword Puzzle Maker
    Allows the user to create a crossword online and print, download to their computer or play online.
    http://www.puzzle-maker.com/CW/
  • Crosswords for Fun
    Learners can become a member of this community and do online crosswords.
    http://www.crosswordsforfun.com.au/pages/default.cfm?page_id=38769
