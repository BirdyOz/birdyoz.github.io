Image embedder & attribution maker (+ YouTube embedder)

for Unsplash, Pexels, Wikimedia Commons, Pixabay and Flickr CC
(Also works with Shutterstock, if you have a paid subscription).

A Bookmarklet to create HTML snippets to embed your selected image, with proper attribution. This can then be pasted into Moodle, or any website that includes Bootstrap 4+.

For those sites NOT running Bootstrap (such as Canvas LMS) a new "Inline CSS" option is now available. This option has fewer features but will work on any website.

First time user?

Follow the instuctions below. Watch the video for step-by-step instructions.

First time user?

  1. Drag the following bookmarklet into your browers bookmark bar: Attribution maker +
    (Note: Make sure you drag the link onto your browser bookmarks. Clicking will just bring you back to here)
  2. Vist any of the supported sites, listed above
  3. Click on the 'Attribution maker' in your bookmarks
  4. You will be returned to this page, but now your selected image will be displayed below
  5. Press the copy button next to the layout type that you prefer. This will copy the HTML to your clipboard
  6. Paste this HTML anywhere that you can paste code (eg a Moodle page or tool)

Important note - Pixabay previews expire in 24hrs

To protect against this, ensure that you:

  1. Download the appropriately sized image
  2. Upload this to Moodle (or other system) to replace the preview
