Assessing online

Using the full potential of e-learning for assessment

When most people think of assessment in e-learning, they think of quizzes - multiple choice, true/false, pick a word. However there is much more to online assessment than multiple choice questions. E-learning offers so many benefits for conducting assessment that we've given it a whole section to itself.

Since assessment is such an integral part of learning design, in the teaching guides we have also described how each e-learning strategy in the Gallery can be used for assessment.
