Unsplash image embedder & attribution maker

A Bookmarklet to create HTML snippets to embed Unsplash images with proper attribution. These can be pasted into any HTML editor.

First time user?

  1. Drag the following bookmarklet into your browers bookmark bar: Unsplash embedder & attribution maker
    (Note: Make sure you drag the link onto your browser bookmarks. Clicking will just bring you back to here)
  2. Visit Unsplash - Beautiful Free Images and find your favourite image
  3. Click on the 'Unsplash embedder & attribution maker' in your bookmarks
  4. You will be returned to this page, but now your selected image will be displayed below
  5. Press the copy button next to the layout type that you prefer. This will copy the HTML to your clipboard
  6. Paste this HTML anywhere that you can paste code (eg a Moodle page or tool)
  7. Ready to go? Visit
