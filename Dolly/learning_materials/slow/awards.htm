E-learning awards

Guides to quality

Until recently, industry e-learning awards internationally have focused on the best ‘package’, or stand-alone content designed for individualised self-paced delivery.

With the growing popularity of blended delivery, more awards are recognising excellence in facilitated e-learning, where the multimedia content is just one part of the design mix which can include collaborative online activities, online support, face-to-face group learning and work-based projects.

Viewing e-learning award-winners can be a useful way to check the state-of-the-art in delivery design as well as in interactive content development.

Resources

  • AIMIA Awards
    http://www.aimia.com.au/i-cms?page=1
    Annual awards from the Australian Interactive Media Industry Association. Although only one category addressing content for education, provides a general insight into current trends in digital content design.
  • LearnX Asia Pacific E-learning and Training Awards

    A range of categories including technologies, courseware and delivery solutions.
