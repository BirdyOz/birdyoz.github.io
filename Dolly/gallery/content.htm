Organise content

Finding and presenting information for learning

The ability to access professionally-designed, reusable learning materials is one of the most obvious benefits of e-learning. You can:

  • Use available learning materials - save time and money with resources such as Flexible Learning Toolboxes (Toolboxes) while your learners benefit from the quality design and engaging content.
  • Use or develop your own resources - use or adapt your current course materials, or prepare materials for online delivery using available tools.
