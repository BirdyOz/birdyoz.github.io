Pixabay attribution helper & image resizer

A Bookmarklet to create a Pixabay attribution plus placeholder image, then properly align these statements with the image.

The placeholder image can then be replaced with a properly resized image, as provided.

First time user?

  1. Drag the following bookmarklet into your browers bookmark bar: Pixabay attribution maker
    (Note: Make sure you drag the link onto your browser bookmarks. Clicking will just bring you back to here)
  2. Visit Pixabay and find your favourite image
  3. Click on the 'Pixabay attribution helper & image resizer' in your bookmarks
  4. You will be returned to this page, but now your selected image will be displayed below
  5. Press the copy button next to the layout type that you prefer. This will copy the HTML to your clipboard
  6. Paste this HTML anywhere that you can paste code (eg a Moodle page or tool)
  7. Download the resized image as provided
  8. Upload this image to replace the placeholder

NB: Pixabay does not allow for hotlinking (embedding). They require that you download their images and re-upload to your own platform (Moodle or other).
