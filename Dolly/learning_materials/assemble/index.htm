Assembling learning materials

Putting existing resources together

Like many teachers and instructors, you may have existing learning materials that you want to use in your e-learning course. These resources may be in formats such as Microsoft Word or PowerPoint files. Rather than starting from scratch, and re-creating these resources, you can convert these into powerful e-learning resources.
