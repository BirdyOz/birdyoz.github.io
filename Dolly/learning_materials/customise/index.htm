Customising learning materials

Making the most of existing resources

Once you have located and downloaded the learning resources you want to include in your e-learning project you may want to customise them. This can make them more specific to your students’ learning needs or you may like to add some of your own content and personality to the resource.

This section gives an overview on how you can customise the following:

  1. Learning objects downloaded from a learning repository.
  2. Web pages that you have either created yourself or obtained from elsewhere.
  3. Photographic images you have taken yourself or downloaded from a web site.
