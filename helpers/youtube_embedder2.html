@BirdyOz: YouTube embedder

YouTube responsive video embedder

A Bookmarklet to create HTML snippets to embed a YouTube video. These can be pasted into any HTML editor.

Videos are fully responsive (scale to 100% of the container). 'Video box' is pre-populated with title, playtime, description and aspect ratio

First time user?

  1. Drag the following bookmarklet into your browers bookmark bar: YouTube Embedder
    (Note: Make sure you drag the link onto your browser bookmarks. Clicking will just bring you back to here)
  2. Visit YouTube and locate the video that you want
  3. Click on the 'Youtube embedder' in your bookmarks
  4. You will be returned to this page, but now your selected video will be displayed below
  5. Press the copy button next to the layout type that you prefer. This will copy the HTML to your clipboard
  6. Paste this HTML anywhere that you can paste code (eg a Moodle page or tool)
